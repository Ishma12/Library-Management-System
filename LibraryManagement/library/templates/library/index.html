Herald College Kathmandu (HCK) provides the best UK university education at most affordable fees. It offers Bachelor Degrees in IT and Business in direct partnership with the University of Wolverhampton, UK. Programmes that Herald College Kathmandu offers are flexible and have always aimed to foster a broader based knowledge.

Herald College Kathmandu offers the BSc (Hons) International Business Management and BSc (Hons) in Computer Science qualification from the University of Wolverhampton (WLV). Considering effort, aptitude and knowledge, Herald College Kathmandu organizes a competition every year where students are awarded with scholarships to participate in 15-Day Summer-Camp organized at the University of Wolverhampton, UK.
